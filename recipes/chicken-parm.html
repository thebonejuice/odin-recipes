Chicken Parmesan

Ingredients For Chicken Parmesan

This recipe is enough for 4 people.

  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • salt and freshly ground pepper to taste
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup panko bread crumbs (whatever you want really.)
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour OR more as needed
  • 1/2 cup olive oil for frying
  • 1/2 cup prepared tomato sauce
  • 1/4 fresh mozzarella
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
  • 1/2 cup grated provolone cheese
  • 2 teaspoons olive oil

Cooking Instructions

  1. Gather ingredients. Preheat over to 450 degrees F
  2. Cover the chicken in plastic and beat the everloving shit out of it
  3. Sprinkle with salt and pepper. Sift flour over the chicken
  4. Beat eggs in a shallow bowl and set aside, Mix crumbs and 1/2 cup parmasan cheese in a separate bowl, set aside.
  5. Dip a flour-coated chicken breast in beaten eggs. Transfer breast to the bread crumb mixture, pressing into both crumbs.
  6. Repeat for each breast. Let chicken rest for 10-15 minutes.
  7. Heat 1/2 inch olive oil in a large skillet on medium-high heat until it begins to shimmer.
  8. Cook chicken in the hot oil until golden, about 2 minutes per side. The chicken will finish cooking in the oven.
  9. Transfer chicken to a baking dish. Top each breast with 2 tablespoons tomato sauce.
  10. Layuer each chicken breast with equal amounts of mozzarella cheese, fresh basil, and provolone cheese. Sprinkle remaining parmesan over top, drizzle with olive oil.
  11. Bake in the preheated oven until cheese is browned and bubbly and chicken breasts are no longer pink in the center, 15-20 minutes. Needs an internnal temp of at least 165 F
  12. Hope that God has mercy on your chicken.
