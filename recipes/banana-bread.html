Banana Bread

Ingredients For Banana Bread.

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Baking Soda
  • Vanilla Extract
  • SOFTENED butter
  • Eggs
  • And of course, mashed bananas
  • Any other ingredients you'd like to put in. Get CRAZY with it.

Cooking Instructions

  1. In a mixing bowl, cream together butter and sugar.
  2. Mix in the mashed bananas.
  3. Whisk together dry ingredients: flour, sugar and baking soda. Add it to the batter.
  4. Stir in the vanilla and other optiona ingredients, then transfer to a prepared loaf pan and bake at 350 F for 30 mins
  5. Pray to God it didn't burn or stick to the pan or it isn't raw.
